Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 1ib penne pasta
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 16oz jar Alfredo-style pasta sauce
  • 1 16oz heavy cream
  • 1 ⅓ cup milk
  • 1 block of parmesan cheese
  • Intalian parsley
  • Land O Lakes half stick of butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt and Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into cubed pieces and season with garlic powder and onion powder.
  2. Fill a pot with water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook penne at a boil until tender yet firm to the bit, about 8 minutes. Drain well.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, cook chicken with any frozen vegetables of you choice. Make sure to butter the pan before use. Add garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper.
  4. While the chicken is cooking, add alfredo sauce, heavy cream, milk, and shredded parmasan cheese to the suacepan with the chicken. Bring to mdeium-low heat. Stir continously
    • Or add pasta into its own sauce pot and cook separately.
  5. Serve warm Alfredo and chicken sauce over cooked pasta.
