Burrito Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1lb block of Velveeta cheese
  • 3lb ground beef
  • 2 packs of burrito mix
  • Chilli pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Lite salt
  • Black pepper
  • 4oz tomato sauce
  • Wolf chilli (no beans)
  • Stick of butter
  • 10in flour burrito tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in a large skillet.
  2. While ground beef is cooking, add onion powder, garlic powder, black pepper, and lite salt. Stir consistently.
  3. When ground beef is brown, strain it. Add burrito mix, 4oz tomato sauce and chilli pepper.
    • Preheat oven to 350° F.
  4. Place burrito in the microwave for 15 seconds and place on the pan when it is done.
    • Make sure to spray foil oil or use a nonstick pan.
  5. Spread cooked meat on tortillas evenly. Fold tortillas like a burrito then place pan in oven.
  6. Bake burrito for 5 minutes, and cook the chilli and cheese simultaneously.
  7. Take burrito out of the oven and spread chilli and cheese 'til your heart's content. Place it back in the oven for another 5 minutes.
  8. Once the burrito is finished, take it out the oven and add any toppings you desire.
