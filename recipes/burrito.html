Burrito Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1lb block of Velveeta cheese
  • 3lb ground beef
  • 2 packs of burrito mix
  • Chilli powder
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Lite salt
  • Black pepper
  • 4oz tomato sauce
  • Wolf chilli (no beans)
  • Stick of butter
  • 10in flower burrito tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in a large skillet.
  2. While ground beef is cooking, add chilli powder, onion powder, garlic powder, black pepper, and lite salt. Stir consistently.
  3. When ground beef is brown, strain it. Add burrito mix, 4oz tomato sauce and Velveeta cheese.
  4. Preheat oven to 350° F.
  5. Place burrito in the microwave for 15 seconds. Place on pan(s).
  6. Spread cooked meat on tortillas evenly. Fold tortillas like a burrito then place pan in oven.
